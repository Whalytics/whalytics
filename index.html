FAQ

Questions traders actually ask.

What exactly does Whalytics track?
Any Solana wallet address you add — transfers, token swaps, and buys/sells. The moment that wallet does something on-chain, you get pinged. You can track known whale wallets or anyone you're curious about.
How fast are the alerts?
On the Pro plan, alerts land within seconds of the on-chain event. The Free plan has a short delay (roughly 10 minutes) so Pro users always see activity first.
Do you have access to my wallet or funds?
No. Whalytics only reads public, on-chain data — the same information anyone can see on a block explorer. We never ask for private keys, seed phrases, or wallet connections. You just paste a public address to watch it.
Which chains are supported?
Solana only, for now — we're keeping the first version focused so alerts stay fast and reliable. Support for additional chains is on the roadmap based on demand.
How is this different from checking Solscan myself?
Solscan works if you're willing to refresh manually and only watch one or two wallets. Whalytics watches unlimited wallets around the clock and pushes alerts straight to Telegram, so you find out the moment something happens instead of after the fact.
What does the Pro plan cost, and how do I pay?
Pro is $19/month for unlimited wallets and real-time alerts. Payment is crypto-only at launch, processed through NOWPayments — no card required.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. There's no lock-in — cancel whenever you like and you won't be charged again the following cycle.
